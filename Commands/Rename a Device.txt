Router: 
    Router(config)#hostname [YOUR DEVICE NEW NAME]
    Router(config)#end

Switch:
    Switch(config)#hostname [YOUR DEVICE NEW NAME]
    Switch(config)#end

Multilayer Switch:
    Switch(config)#hostname [YOUR DEVICE NEW NAME]
    Switch(config)#end

Example:
    Router: 
        Router(config)#hostname A1-R
        A1-R(config)#end

    Switch:
        Switch(config)#hostname A2-Sw
        A2-Sw(config)#end

        Multilayer Switch:
        Switch(config)#hostname B5-MS
        B5-MS(config)#end
